Frequently Asked Questions

Do you offer free inspections?+

Yes — 100% free, zero obligation. We give honest feedback on whether you need a repair, replacement, or nothing at all.

Will my insurance premium go up?+

In Arkansas, insurers generally cannot raise your rates for an "Act of God" storm claim. We walk you through every step.

How long does a full replacement take?+

Most Little Rock homes are done in a single day. Larger or complex roofs may take two days.

What shingle materials do you use?+

Class-4 impact-resistant architectural shingles from Owens Corning and GAF — both carry lifetime limited warranties.

Do you clean up after installation?+

Absolutely. We tarp the property, haul all debris, and run magnetic sweepers across your yard — we leave it cleaner than we found it.

Do you offer financing?+

Yes! We offer 0% APR promotional periods and low monthly payments so a new roof is always affordable.

Are you licensed and insured in Arkansas?+

Yes. We carry full general liability and workers' comp insurance and hold all required Arkansas contractor licenses.
